Project 09: Vinyl Cutting

]

For my 15 character phrase I decided to search up quotes by Bob Marley. Originally this quote was in jpeg and I needed to convert it into png.

I imported this image into Adobe Illustrator and then I realized that I didn't like the font of this quote. Following that, I decided to search up another image and import it into Adobe Illustrator.

The image/text were first saved on the computer and then converted into a png file. I uploaded it using the Clip Art tool in the computer software, Sure Cuts A Lot. I selected the entire image to merge it. It was then mirrored and placed in the top right corner of the artboard. I pressed the scissors icon

I made sure that the vinyl cutter was on the correct speed and force fro glitter vinyl. I used 84mm/s as my speed and 135g as my force.

I decided to put the 16 character text on the front of my hoodie and the image of Bob Marley on the back of it.

This is the final product!!
