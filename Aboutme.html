About Me

Ibraheem

My name is Ibraheem Ayinde, I'm currently a sophomore at Wheaton College and I work at the den. I really value all types of art, whether it be music, performing, or visual. I love to do them all. I am an ambitious person that can't take no for an answer.

I remember working at a uniform retail store in Bronx, NY during my junior year of high school and my job was to make school uniforms using machinery such as the silk screen press and an embroidery machine. It instanly caught my attention and I enjoyed working at that particullar job for that reason. I was able to work my way into being able to teach other workers how to use it within a weeks' time of using it.

I would not be where I am today if I did not take any risks and found my way how to complete certain tasks on my own. I am a pretty sociable person, I am always looking for something new to do. As you can already tell, sometimes can be all over the place but it is a thought process that has helped me be more organized and also not to be uniterested in many things. I also value the time spent with people, whether it be my family or friends. It is important to me to show love to the people I care for and to also try and learn something new everyday. I really would like to see how much I am going to learn in this course by the end of the semester. I want to be able to retain this information and possibly teach it to my friends as well.

If you are reading this, I truly hope you have a great day!
